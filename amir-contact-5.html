FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to the questions Amir gets asked most often — from collaborators, journalists, students and policymakers.

Are you available for speaking engagements?
Yes, I regularly contribute to conferences, panels, and workshops focused on AI ethics, governance, and autonomous systems. Please include event details, audience, and format in your enquiry.
Can you collaborate on research projects?
I welcome research collaborations aligned with my areas of expertise. Please include details of the project scope, objectives, and expected contributions.
Do you work with policymakers and regulators?
Yes. My research focuses on the intersection of ethical theory and practical regulatory frameworks. I engage with policy discussions relating to autonomous systems, safety standards, and ethical implementation.
Do you work with students and early career researchers?
Yes, I enjoy supporting emerging researchers in AI ethics and autonomous systems. Whether you're looking for guidance on your thesis, a reading list, or general career advice, feel free to send a message. Response time may be slower for informal academic mentoring, but I will reply to genuine enquiries.
Do you provide consulting or advisory services?
I am open to advisory engagements that align with my research focus, particularly where ethical frameworks can be applied to real-world systems.
